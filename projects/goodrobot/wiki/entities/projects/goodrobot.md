## Problem
SMBs (10-200 employees) struggle to deploy AI agents without engineering resources. Gap between toy assistants and enterprise platforms too expensive/complex for mid-market.

## Value Proposition
Plug-and-play AI agent teams that integrate with existing workflows in hours, not months. Pre-built agent roles (sales, operations, finance, marketing) that learn your business and operate autonomously.

## Target Market
