# Feedforward Activity

**Feedforward activity** refers to the bottom-up flow of information in the nervous system. In the visual system, this is the process of light entering the eyes and being transformed into neural signals that travel through a sequence of increasingly complex brain regions.

## Process

1. **Early Visual Cortex:** Detects edges, lines, and simple features.
2. **Intermediate Regions:** Combine features into shapes.
3. **High-Level Regions:** Assemble shapes into recognizable objects, faces, and scenes.
