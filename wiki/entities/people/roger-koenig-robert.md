# Roger Koenig-Robert

Roger Koenig-Robert is a neuroscientist known for his research on the neural substrates of visual imagery and the [[spontaneous-activity-reshaping-hypothesis]].

## Contributions

- **Imagination Theory:** Proposes that imagination is a process of sculpting mental images from background neural activity through targeted suppression.
- **Experimental Work:** Conducted experiments showing that mental imagery footprints on behavior match neural suppression rather than active firing.
