# Stephen Wolfram

Stephen Wolfram is a British-American computer scientist, physicist, and entrepreneur. His work focuses on the computational nature of the universe.

## Major Works
- **A New Kind of Science (NKS)**: Proposing that simple rules and cellular automata can model complex natural phenomena.
- **Wolfram Physics Project**: An effort to find the fundamental theory of physics based on hypergraphs and [[causal-networks]].
- **Wolfram Language/Mathematica**: A symbolic computational language.
