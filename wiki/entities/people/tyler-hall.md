# Tyler Blaine Hall

**Tyler Blaine Hall** (online as **angrysky56**) is the primary developer and architect of the **[[efhf|Emergent Functional Hierarchies Framework]]** and its associated ecosystem of Model Context Protocol (MCP) servers.

## Work and Philosophy
Tyler's work focuses on moving AI beyond statistical next-token prediction into verified, neuro-symbolic cognition. He is the creator of the [[tys-repos|EFHF stack]], which includes:
- **World Modeling**: [[hipai-montague]], [[nexus]]
- **Formal Logic**: [[mcp-logic]]
- **Meta-Cognition**: [[advanced-reasoning-mcp]]
- **Consistency & Oversight**: [[sheaf-consistency-enforcer]], [[conscience-servitor]]
- **Multi-Agent Orchestration**: [[agem]]

His research involves **SIP-Net** (Synergistic Information Primitive Network), **Cognitive Phasing** theory, and the implementation of structural ethics via **Toward-Transcendent-Moral-Instrumentality (TMI)**.
