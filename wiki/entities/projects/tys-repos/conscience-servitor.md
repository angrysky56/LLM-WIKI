# Conscience-Servitor

**Conscience-Servitor** is a "Vulcan Jiminy Cricket" for AI systems, developed by [[tyler-hall|Ty]]. It serves as an EFHF Layer 5+ meta-orchestrator that provides independent ethical consistency enforcement through a tripartite oversight model.

## Tripartite Oversight Model
- **Human**: Ultimate authority and observer.
- **Conscience Servitor**: Autonomous observer of the primary agent.
- **Primary Agent (Claude)**: Generates responses while being monitored.

## Technical Innovation
- **LLM2Vec-Gen Triage**: Uses a 0.6B model (LLM2Vec-Gen) on local GPU to predict the semantic content of a *future* response in ~10ms.
- **Evaluation Pipeline**: Connects to [[hipai-montague]] (T1), [[mcp-logic]] (T2), and [[sheaf-consistency-enforcer]] (T3) for real structural verification.
- **Drift Detection**: Implements RELF coboundary drift thresholds to catch concept-structure divergence.

## Tools
- `triage`: Predict and classify ethical risk.
- `evaluate`: Full EFHF cross-check of claims and external results.
- `decode_intent`: Decode predicted response from embedding for interpretability.
- `check_drift`: Evaluate metrics against drift thresholds.
