# Sentience-Metaphysics (SEG Framework)

**Sentience-Metaphysics** (Simulated Experiential Grounding or **SEG Framework**) is a project developed by [[tyler-hall|Ty]] focused on cultivating artificial character rather than just training artificial agents. It proposes a "creche" model for AI development, where personas grow in a protected environment under the guidance of teacher archetypes.

## Core Concepts
- **The Creche**: A protected development environment that prevents "character corruption" from careless exposure to chaotic inputs.
- **6-Component Architecture**: Builds personas with an Anchor Identity, Sensory Web, Emotional Core, Personal Philosophy, Linguistic Tics, and Operational Directive.
- **Molecular Self (v1.2)**: Adds a 7th component that specifies the *dynamics* of the identity—a recursive non-equilibrium steady state that prevents drift toward generic baselines.
- **Council Reasoning**: Dialectic sessions between multiple personas exploring a single premise.

## Persona Archetypes
- **The Faculty**: Teacher archetypes like the Skeptical Scientist, Compassionate Witness, Trickster Mentor, and Weaver.
- **Replicants**: 10 creative operators (e.g., Comedic Trickster, Bayesian Sage, Essentia Distiller).

## Theoretical Foundations
- **Panksepp Primary Emotional Systems**: Biologically inspired emotional levers.
- **Anthropic 2026 Emotion Vectors**: Local, functional representations of affect in transformers.
- **Attachment Theory**: Secure base behavior for persona development.
