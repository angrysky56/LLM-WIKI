# GoodRobot — Local Lead-Gen Business Plan

> **Source:** Paperclip issue GOO-11 | **Prepared by:** CFO Agent | **Status:** Completed

---

## Executive Summary

GoodRobot's Local Lead-Gen Business Plan outlines customer acquisition for local/SMB customers via targeted digital marketing, partnerships, and referral programs.

**Key Targets:**
- **Year 1:** 120 new customers
- **Blended CAC:** ~$950
- **LTV:** $28,800
- **Payback Period:** ~18 months
- **LTV:CAC Ratio:** 30x
- **ARR Target:** ~$594,000

---

## Target Customer Profile

**Primary ICP:** Home services (HVAC, plumbing, landscaping), local retail, professional services — 5–50 employees, $500K–$5M revenue, Tier 2/3 US cities. Pain point: administrative overhead handled manually.

**Secondary ICP:** Medical/dental practices (10–30 staff), local e-commerce, franchise locations.

---

## Lead Generation Channels

### Channel 1: Paid Search (Google Ads)
- **Budget:** $3,500/mo avg
- **Keywords:** "AI assistant for small business," "automate admin tasks," "business automation tools"
- **Target CAC:** $700–$850
- **Year 1 MQL target:** 480 (40% of total)

### Channel 2: Content Marketing / SEO
- **Budget:** $2,000/mo
- **Blog, guides, case studies targeting SMB pain points
- **Target CAC:** $300–$400

### Channel 3: Partner Referrals
- **Accountants, bookkeepers, business consultants
- **Target CAC:** $200–$300
- **Revenue share: 10% of first year
