# Eris (Mythology)

**Source:** Wikipedia
**Type:** Reference — mythology

## Summary

Eris is the Greek goddess of strife and discord. Her most famous mythological appearance is at the wedding of Peleus and Thetis, where she threw the Apple of Discord ("for the fairest") among the goddesses, triggering the events that led to the Trojan War.
